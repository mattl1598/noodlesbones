NoodlesBones.com is your one-stop shop for quick and easy updates about Noodle the Pug and the status of his bones. We try to update the site within an hour of a new round of No Bones being released.

There are a handful of other features that we aim to bring in the next few weeks.
Roadmap:

  • The Noodles Forecast (an AI guess as to the result of tomorrows video) (Currently live on the homepage for testing).
  • A Notification System for video releases and the result of todays No Bones.
  • Google Assistant integration so you can ask Google if its a Bones or No Bones day.
  • A public NoodlesBones API for programmers to play around with.
  • And More!
